Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2025

Overview

Nudge is a minimalist time reminder app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: none).

Data Collection

Nudge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements
  • No data sent to external servers

Data Storage

All app settings (interval preferences, audio channel selection, language) are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

  • On Android: Settings are stored in SharedPreferences and backed up via Android Auto Backup
  • On iOS: Settings are stored locally with Keychain backup for persistence

Permissions

Nudge requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send periodic reminder beeps, even when the app is closed
  • Exact Alarms (Android): To ensure reminders are delivered at precise times

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the app.

Children's Privacy

Nudge does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Nudge does not use any third-party services that collect data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
